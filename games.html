Célula

A roguelike game I'm currently making

Programming

The game was made in GameMaker Studio 2, with gml and lua, (gml being GameMakers local programming language), and lua is an extension by Apollo, which makes the game easily moddable, like adding new weapons, mutations, etc

Art

art is made in paint.net, an outdated upgrade of ms paint, even if i tried to use modern applications my art would still be in the same category xD

Music

i LOVE music, i listen to music while waiting, eating, sleeping, but my favorite activity is to look at the sky at night, listening to my favorite bands



my taste in music is pretty much just metal, even though i sometimes listen to some hiphop/trap

the game ost is inspired by industrial metal bands like, Nine Inch Nails, Marilyin Manson, pitchshifter, Powerman 5000, Fear Factory, Sybreed and Charlie Clouser (composer of the movie Saw). Nine Inch Nails being the most influencial, i guess

my original plan was to make a doom metal ost, with slow, low tuned, distorted guitars, but in my opinion, making living organisms sound industrial gave it a different vibe i wouldn't get with doom metal


you can listen to the ost on my bandcamp page, or down below! :D
